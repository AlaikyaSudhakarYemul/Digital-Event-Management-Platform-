## 1. Introduction
The DEMP frontend is a modern web application built with React, designed for seamless event discovery, ticket booking, and event management. It is accessible on desktop and mobile browsers.

## 2. Getting Started
- Open the DEMP application in your browser.
- For best results, use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
- Ensure a stable internet connection.

## 3. User Registration
- Click "Sign Up" on the homepage.
- Fill in your username, email, and password (minimum 8 characters, mix of letters and numbers).
- Agree to the terms and submit.
- You will receive a confirmation email. Click the link to activate your account.

## 4. User Login & Password Reset
- Click "Login" and enter your email and password.
- If you forget your password, click "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions sent to your email.
- After login, you are redirected to your dashboard.

## 5. Profile Management
- Click your avatar or username in the top navigation bar.
- Update your personal details, change your password, and set notification preferences.
- To delete your account, use the "Delete Account" option (irreversible).

## 6. Browsing & Searching Events
- Browse featured and upcoming events on the homepage.
- Use the search bar to find events by name, date, or location.
- Filter events by category, date range, or popularity.
- Click an event card for more details.

## 7. Event Details Page
- View event description, date, time, venue, ticket types, and prices.
- See event images, organizer info, and attendee reviews (if available).
- Use the "Share" button to send event details to friends.

## 8. Booking Tickets
- On the event page, select ticket type and quantity.
- Click "Book Now" to review your selection.
- If not logged in, you will be prompted to log in or register.
- Confirm your booking to proceed to payment.

## 9. Making Payments
- Choose your payment method (credit/debit card, UPI, etc.).
- Enter payment details and complete the transaction securely.
- On success, you receive a confirmation message and email.

## 10. My Tickets & Downloads
- Access "My Tickets" from the main menu.
- View all your booked tickets, event details, and status.
- Download tickets as PDF for printing or mobile use.
- Show the QR code at the event for entry.

## 11. Notifications
- Receive notifications for booking confirmations, event updates, and reminders.
- Access notifications via the bell icon in the navigation bar.
- Mark notifications as read or delete them as needed.

## 12. Event Management (Organizers)
- Organizers can access the "Organizer Dashboard" from the main menu.
- Click "Create Event" to add a new event: enter title, description, date, venue, ticket types, pricing, and upload images.
- Set ticket sales periods and special conditions (e.g., early bird pricing).
- Publish the event to make it live.

## 13. Organizer Dashboard
- View and manage all your events in one place.
- Edit event details, update ticket availability, or cancel events.
- Monitor ticket sales in real time.
- Download attendee lists as CSV.
- Send notifications to attendees.

## 14. Analytics & Reports (Organizers)
- Access analytics for each event: tickets sold, revenue, attendee demographics.
- Visualize trends with charts (sales over time, peak booking periods).
- Export reports for further analysis.

## 15. Attendee Management (Organizers)
- View and search attendee lists for each event.
- Mark attendance, resend tickets, or process refunds.
- Handle special requests (e.g., accessibility, VIP upgrades).

## 16. Security & Privacy
- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).
- Passwords are securely hashed.
- Personal data is handled per privacy policy and not shared with third parties.
- Log out after each session, especially on shared devices.

## 17. Troubleshooting & Support
- For login or payment issues, check your credentials and internet connection.
- If you do not receive your ticket, check your spam folder or download from "My Tickets".
- Use the in-app support chat or email support@demp.example.com for help.
- Refresh the page or log out and back in for minor issues.

## 18. FAQ
- **How do I reset my password?** Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
- **Can I transfer my ticket?** Ticket transfer is available for select events. Contact support for details.
- **What payment methods are accepted?** Credit/debit cards, UPI, and more.
- **How do I contact support?** Use the in-app chat or email support@demp.example.com.
- **Is my data secure?** Yes, all data is encrypted and handled securely.
