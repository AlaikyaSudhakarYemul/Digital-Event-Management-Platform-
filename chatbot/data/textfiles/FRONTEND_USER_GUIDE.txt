# Frontend User Guide

This document provides a comprehensive guide for end users of the Digital Event Management Platform (DEMP) React frontend application. It covers all major features, user flows, and best practices for interacting with the platform.

---

## Project Overview
The Digital Event Management Platform (DEMP) is a full-featured, cloud-ready solution for managing events, ticketing, payments, and user engagement. The frontend is built with React and integrates seamlessly with a robust Java/Spring Boot backend and a Python-based AI chatbot. The platform is designed for scalability, security, and a modern user experience.

### Key Features
- Event discovery and advanced search
- Secure user registration, login, and profile management
- Real-time ticket booking and seat selection
- Integrated payment gateway (PCI-compliant)
- Notifications via email, SMS, and in-app alerts
- Downloadable e-tickets (PDF)
- Admin dashboard for event organizers
- Analytics and reporting for event performance
- AI-powered chatbot for user support and FAQs

### Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript (ES6+), Axios
- **Backend:** Java 17, Spring Boot 3.x, REST APIs
- **Chatbot:** Python 3.10+, FastAPI, LangChain, ChromaDB
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (primary), Redis (caching)
- **Authentication:** OAuth2/JWT
- **Deployment:** Docker, Azure/AWS

### User Roles
- **Attendee:** Register, browse events, book tickets, make payments, receive notifications
- **Organizer/Admin:** Create/manage events, view analytics, manage users, access admin dashboard

### UI/UX Highlights
- Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Intuitive navigation with clear call-to-actions
- Accessible forms with validation and helpful error messages
- Real-time feedback for actions (loading spinners, toasts, confirmations)
- Dark mode support (if enabled)

### Integration Points
- **Backend API:** All user actions (registration, booking, payments) are processed via secure REST endpoints
- **Chatbot:** Embedded AI assistant for instant help, event search, and booking guidance
- **Payment Gateway:** Secure, PCI-compliant payment processing
- **Notifications:** Real-time updates via email, SMS, and in-app

### Advanced Usage
- **Bulk Booking:** Organizers can enable group ticket purchases for corporate or VIP events
- **Event Reminders:** Users receive automated reminders for upcoming events
- **Custom Reports:** Organizers can generate downloadable reports on ticket sales and attendance
- **Multi-language Support:** (Planned) Interface and notifications in multiple languages

### Security & Privacy
- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- User credentials are securely hashed and never stored in plain text
- Strict RBAC ensures only authorized users can access admin features
- Compliance with GDPR and PCI standards

### Support & Feedback
- In-app support chat for real-time help
- Email support at support@demp.example.com
- Feedback form for suggestions and bug reports

## 1. Introduction
The DEMP frontend is a modern, responsive web application built with React. It provides a seamless interface for users to discover events, book tickets, make payments, and manage their profiles.

**What does this mean for you?**
- You can easily find and register for events online.
- All actions, from searching to payment, happen in one place.
- The interface is designed to be simple and intuitive, even for first-time users.

**Example:**
If you want to attend a tech conference, you can search for "Tech Conference" on the homepage, view event details, book your ticket, pay securely, and download your ticket—all within a few clicks.

## 2. Getting Started
- Open the DEMP application in your web browser.
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
- For the best experience, use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.

**Tip:** Bookmark the DEMP homepage for quick access.

## 3. User Registration & Login
- **Register:** Click on the "Sign Up" button and fill in your username, email, and password. Submit the form to create your account.
- **Login:** Click on the "Login" button and enter your credentials. Upon successful authentication, you will be redirected to the dashboard.
- **Password Reset:** Use the "Forgot Password" link to reset your password via email.

**Note:** Your password must be at least 8 characters long and contain a mix of letters and numbers for security.

## 4. Profile Management
- Access your profile by clicking on your username/avatar in the top navigation bar.
- Update your personal information, change your password, and manage notification preferences.
- Delete your account if you no longer wish to use the platform (irreversible action).

**Example:**
To change your email address, go to your profile, edit the email field, and save changes. You will receive a confirmation email to verify the new address.

## 5. Browsing & Searching Events
- Browse featured and upcoming events on the homepage.
- Use the search bar to find events by name, date, or location.
- Filter events by category, date range, or popularity.
- Click on an event card to view detailed information, including description, date, venue, and available tickets.

**Tip:** Use filters to quickly narrow down events that match your interests or schedule.

## 6. Booking Tickets
- On the event details page, select the number of tickets and ticket type (if applicable).
- Click "Book Now" to proceed to the booking summary.
- Review your selection and confirm the booking.
- If required, log in or register before completing the booking.

**Example:**
If you want to book 2 VIP tickets for a concert, select "VIP" as the ticket type, enter "2" as the quantity, and follow the prompts to complete your booking.

## 7. Making Payments
- After confirming your ticket selection, proceed to the payment page.
- Choose your preferred payment method (credit/debit card, UPI, etc.).
- Enter payment details and complete the transaction securely.
- Upon successful payment, you will receive a confirmation message and email.

**Security:**
All payments are processed through a secure, PCI-compliant gateway. Your card details are never stored on the platform.

## 8. Notifications & Ticket Downloads
- View notifications for booking confirmations, event updates, and reminders in the notifications panel.
- Download your tickets as PDF files from the "My Tickets" section.
- Receive email notifications for all important actions.

**Example:**
After booking, you will see a notification in the app and receive an email with your ticket attached as a PDF. You can also download it anytime from "My Tickets."

## 9. Security & Privacy
- All user data and transactions are secured with HTTPS.
- Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text.
- Personal information is handled in accordance with privacy policies and regulations.
- Log out after each session, especially on shared devices.

**Your privacy matters:**
We do not share your personal data with third parties. You can review our privacy policy from the app footer.

## 10. Troubleshooting & Support
- **Common Issues:**
  - Login problems: Check your credentials and internet connection.
  - Payment failures: Ensure your payment details are correct and try again.
  - Ticket not received: Check your email spam folder or download from "My Tickets".
- **Support Channels:**
  - Use the in-app support chat or contact support@demp.example.com for assistance.

**Quick Fix:**
If you encounter any issue, try refreshing the page or logging out and back in. For persistent problems, contact support with a description and screenshot if possible.

## Appendix: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

**Q: Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?**
A: Ticket transfer is supported for select events. Check the event details or contact support for assistance.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Credit/debit cards, UPI, and other region-specific methods are supported via the integrated payment gateway.

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Use the in-app chat or email support@demp.example.com.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and handled according to industry best practices and compliance standards.
