# DEMP Frontend Analytics & Reports (Organizers) Deep Dive

This document provides a comprehensive guide and technical overview of the analytics and reporting features available to organizers in the Digital Event Management Platform (DEMP) frontend application. It covers analytics dashboards, report generation, data visualization, validation, troubleshooting, backend integration, and best practices.

---

## 1. Overview & Purpose
- The analytics and reports module empowers organizers to monitor event performance, ticket sales, attendee demographics, and revenue trends.
- Enables data-driven decision-making and post-event analysis.

---

## 2. Analytics Dashboard Features
- **Sales Overview:**
  - Total tickets sold, revenue generated, and sales breakdown by ticket type.
- **Attendee Demographics:**
  - Insights into attendee age, location, and other available attributes.
- **Sales Trends:**
  - Visual charts showing sales over time, peak booking periods, and conversion rates.
- **Engagement Metrics:**
  - Page views, shares, and other engagement indicators (if tracked).
- **Real-Time Updates:**
  - Live data refresh for ongoing events.

---

## 3. Reports & Export Tools
- **Downloadable Reports:**
  - Export ticket sales, attendee lists, and revenue data as CSV or PDF.
- **Custom Reports:**
  - Filter and generate reports for specific date ranges, ticket types, or attendee segments.
- **Post-Event Summary:**
  - Automated summary report after event completion, including key metrics and feedback.

---

## 4. Data Visualization
- **Charts & Graphs:**
  - Bar, line, and pie charts for sales, demographics, and engagement.
- **Interactive Filters:**
  - Adjust date ranges, ticket types, or other parameters to update visualizations.
- **Comparative Analysis:**
  - Compare performance across multiple events.

---

## 5. Validation & Error Handling
- **Frontend Validation:**
  - Ensures only authorized organizers can access analytics.
  - Validates filter inputs and report parameters.
- **Backend Validation:**
  - Confirms data accuracy and permissions.
- **Error Feedback:**
  - Clear messages for failed data loads, export errors, or permission issues.

---

## 6. Integration with Backend
- **API Endpoints:**
  - `GET /api/organizer/analytics/{eventId}` — Fetch analytics for a specific event.
  - `GET /api/organizer/reports/{eventId}` — Download reports for an event.
- **Sample Response (Analytics):**
  ```json
  {
    "ticketsSold": 120,
    "revenue": 11880,
    "attendees": [
      {"age": 28, "location": "NY"},
      ...
    ],
    "salesTrends": [
      {"date": "2026-05-01", "tickets": 20},
      ...
    ]
  }
  ```

---

## 7. Security & Permissions
- Only authenticated users with organizer roles can access analytics and reports.
- All report downloads are logged for audit purposes.

---

## 8. Troubleshooting & Support
- **Data Not Updating:**
  - Refresh the dashboard or check your internet connection.
- **Export Issues:**
  - Try a different browser or contact support if downloads fail.
- **Support:**
  - Use in-app chat or email support@demp.example.com for help.

---

## 9. Best Practices
- Regularly review analytics to optimize event marketing and operations.
- Export and archive reports for record-keeping and compliance.
- Use comparative analysis to improve future events.
