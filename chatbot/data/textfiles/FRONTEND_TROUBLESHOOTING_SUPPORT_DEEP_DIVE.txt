# DEMP Frontend Troubleshooting & Support Deep Dive

This document provides a comprehensive guide and technical overview of troubleshooting and support features in the Digital Event Management Platform (DEMP) frontend application. It covers common issues, self-service steps, support channels, validation, escalation, backend integration, and best practices.

---

## 1. Overview & Purpose
- The troubleshooting and support module helps users resolve common issues and access timely assistance.
- Provides self-service solutions and direct support channels for both attendees and organizers.

---

## 2. Common Issues & Solutions
- **Login Problems:**
  - Check email and password for typos.
  - Use "Forgot Password" to reset credentials.
  - Ensure internet connectivity.
- **Registration Issues:**
  - Verify email format and password requirements.
  - Check for existing account with the same email.
- **Payment Failures:**
  - Confirm card/UPI details and sufficient funds.
  - Try a different payment method or browser.
  - Contact support if double-charged.
- **Ticket Not Received:**
  - Check spam/junk folder.
  - Download from "My Tickets" section.
  - Resend ticket from attendee management (organizers).
- **Event Access Issues:**
  - Ensure QR code is visible and undamaged.
  - Arrive early and contact event staff if problems persist.
- **Page Loading Errors:**
  - Refresh the page or clear browser cache.
  - Try a different browser or device.

---

## 3. Self-Service Tools
- **In-App Help:**
  - Access help articles and FAQs from the support menu.
- **Password Reset:**
  - Use "Forgot Password" on the login page.
- **Ticket Download:**
  - Download tickets anytime from "My Tickets".
- **Notification Center:**
  - Review recent updates and alerts for known issues.

---

## 4. Support Channels
- **In-App Chat:**
  - Real-time chat with support agents for urgent issues.
- **Email Support:**
  - Contact support@demp.example.com for non-urgent queries.
- **Escalation:**
  - Critical issues (e.g., payment failures, account lockout) are prioritized.

---

## 5. Validation & Error Handling
- **Frontend Validation:**
  - Provides clear, actionable error messages for all user actions.
- **Backend Validation:**
  - Ensures accurate error reporting and logs issues for support review.
- **Error Feedback:**
  - Suggests next steps or directs users to support channels.

---

## 6. Integration with Backend
- **API Endpoints:**
  - Support requests and chat are logged and tracked via backend APIs.
  - Error logs are correlated with user sessions for faster resolution.

---

## 7. Escalation & Follow-Up
- **Ticketing System:**
  - Support requests are assigned ticket numbers for tracking.
- **Status Updates:**
  - Users receive updates on the progress and resolution of their issues.
- **Feedback:**
  - Users can rate support interactions and provide feedback for improvement.

---

## 8. Best Practices
- Try self-service solutions before contacting support.
- Provide detailed information (screenshots, error messages) when raising issues.
- Keep contact information up to date for timely support.
