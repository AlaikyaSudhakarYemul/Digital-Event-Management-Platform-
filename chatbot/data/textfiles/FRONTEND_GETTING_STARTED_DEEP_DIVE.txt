# DEMP Frontend Getting Started Deep Dive

This document provides an expanded, step-by-step guide to getting started with the Digital Event Management Platform (DEMP) frontend application. It covers access, environment requirements, first-time setup, navigation, and troubleshooting for new users and organizers.

---

## 1. Accessing the Application
- Open your preferred web browser (latest Chrome, Firefox, or Edge recommended).
- Navigate to the DEMP application URL (provided by your organization or event host).
- Ensure a stable internet connection for best performance.

---

## 2. Supported Devices & Browsers
- **Desktop:** Windows, macOS, Linux (modern browsers)
- **Mobile:** Android and iOS (Chrome, Safari, Edge)
- **Tablet:** Fully responsive layouts for tablets
- **Browser Requirements:**
  - JavaScript enabled
  - Cookies enabled (for authentication)
  - Pop-ups allowed for payment flows (if required)

---

## 3. First-Time User Checklist
- Bookmark the DEMP application URL for easy access.
- Review the privacy policy and terms of service.
- Prepare your email and a secure password for registration.
- (Organizers) Gather event details and images for event creation.

---

## 4. Application Layout Overview
- **Top Navigation Bar:** Access to login, registration, profile, notifications, and main menu.
- **Homepage:** Featured and upcoming events, search bar, and filters.
- **Sidebar/Menu:** Quick links to "My Tickets", "Organizer Dashboard", and support.
- **Footer:** Contact info, support links, and legal notices.

---

## 5. User Onboarding Flow
- **Step 1:** Register a new account or log in with existing credentials.
- **Step 2:** Complete your profile (optional, but recommended for personalized experience).
- **Step 3:** Browse events, use filters/search to find events of interest.
- **Step 4:** Book tickets and complete payment securely.
- **Step 5:** Access "My Tickets" for downloads and event entry.

---

## 6. Organizer Onboarding Flow
- **Step 1:** Register as an organizer (or upgrade your user account).
- **Step 2:** Access the "Organizer Dashboard" from the main menu.
- **Step 3:** Click "Create Event" and fill in event details, ticket types, and upload images.
- **Step 4:** Publish your event and monitor ticket sales in real time.
- **Step 5:** Use analytics and attendee management features as needed.

---

## 7. Troubleshooting Common Issues
- **Login Problems:**
  - Check your credentials and internet connection.
  - Use the "Forgot Password" link if needed.
- **Payment Issues:**
  - Ensure pop-ups are enabled and payment details are correct.
  - Contact support if payment fails repeatedly.
- **Display/Navigation Issues:**
  - Refresh the page or clear browser cache.
  - Try a different browser if problems persist.
- **Support:**
  - Use the in-app chat or email support@demp.example.com for help.

---

## 8. Best Practices
- Log out after each session, especially on shared devices.
- Keep your browser updated for security and compatibility.
- Use strong, unique passwords for your account.
